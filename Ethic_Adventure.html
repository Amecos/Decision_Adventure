You are a young adventurer named Jalen who has embarked on a quest to navigate the complexities of decision-making. Your choices will determine your character's moral compass and influence the world around you. HelpStranger

You decide to help the stranger, who appears to be injured. As you approach, you realize the stranger is being chased by a dangerous creature. You have two options: [[Fight the creature to protect the stranger|FightCreature]] [[Leave the area to avoid danger|LeaveArea]]
